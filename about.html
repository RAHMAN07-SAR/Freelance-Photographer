My Story

Documenting life’s most authentic moments with passion and creativity.

Meet Ananya

I’m a freelance photographer based in Bengaluru, India. Over the last decade, I’ve developed a signature style blending documentary and fine-art techniques to produce photographs that are soulful and timeless.

My expertise ranges from weddings and couple sessions to brand campaigns and editorial portraits. Every assignment is a fresh opportunity to create magic with light and composition.

See My Work

Journey So Far

2013 – First Camera

Bought my first DSLR during college. Experimented with portraits and street photography, learning the basics through trial and error.

2018 – Exploring Travel Photography

Travelled across India and Southeast Asia, capturing landscapes and cultures. This experience shaped my visual storytelling style.

2021 – Full-Time Photographer

Turned my passion into a profession. Started VisionFrames Photography and began working with clients for weddings, portraits, and commercial projects.
